Tertiary Education in the world

The number of people who attend university has been increasing every year. In Australia, around 11% of the population had been to university in 1970. In 2010, that has increased to over 18%.

In 2010, South Korea had the highest percentage of people who atteneded university of 30%!

Tertiary Education in the Australia

In 2019, Business, administration and law had the highest percentage of graduates with over 36% of the total graduates.

Graduates for all levels of tertiary education seems to be rising. The number of Masters graduates seems to rising the most with an over 100% growth from 2005 to 2019
